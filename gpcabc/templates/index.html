Join or update your info with the Alumni Association

Thank you for your continued interest in Govt. Polytechnin College Bikaner! Click the button below to join or update your information to activate your Alumni Association membership. Once submitted, you will be mailed: Govt. Polytechnin College Alumni Association welcome packet Govt. Polytechnin College Alumni Association Member I.D. card* (to access on-campus benefits like the fitness center, library, and other benefits) *To receive an Alumni I.D. card, you must provide either your former Govt. Polytechnin College Student I.D. number or the last 4 digits of your SSN in the online form. You may request your former Student I.D. number by phone or in person by providing your Social Security number to the Govt. Polytechnin College Records Department. Please call 8003781633 for assistance. The information provided will not be shared with any outside organization.


Share your story

Govt. Polytechnin College transforms lives through innovative, rigorous, and compassionate education. You came here to discover what’s next – now, share your triumphs with us, and tell us how Govt. Polytechnin College has helped you transform your life!


Attend an Alumni event

Meet up with old friends or make new ones at various networking and social events for alumni. Come back to campus for educational and cultural events, and to support our sports teams (Go Wood Ducks!)



Join the Alumni Association Board of Directors

A volunteer Board of Directors comprised of alumni, community leaders, faculty, and staff governs the Govt. Polytechnin College Alumni Association (PCAA). We select members based on their dedication to strengthening the bonds between alumni and our community while furthering the mission of Govt. Polytechnin College. Our mission is to mobilize and support current students and alumni through educational, cultural, social networking, and service-related opportunities.



Find a job

Alumni can access the Career Services center for help with resume development, job search assistance, interviewing techniques, and onsite job fairs. Alumni are also able to search for jobs on Govt. Polytechnin College's Central Network (CCN), which features job postings by employers specifically looking to hire Govt. Polytechnin College grads, and includes more resources for career advice like podcasts and a weekly email update with tips and trends.



Give back

Our alumni are some of Govt. Polytechnin College's biggest supporters. Those who are able to give can choose to do so in a variety of ways - monetary donations, gifts in kind, securities and stocks, and legacy planning. If you are planning a gift, check to see if your company offers a match.
